1. Acceptance and eligibility

By creating an account, booking a game, earning credits, or otherwise using PlayCred, you agree to the user terms and the privacy policy that apply to the platform.

PlayCred is intended only for users who are 18 years old or older. If PlayCred learns that an account belongs to someone under 18, it may suspend or delete that account where allowed by law.

2. Accounts and security

You must provide accurate, complete, and current information when you register and keep your details up to date.

You are responsible for protecting your login details and for all activity that happens through your account. PlayCred may use security checks, fraud-prevention tools, and device or log data to protect the platform and its users.

PlayCred may suspend or refuse access if it detects false, duplicate, abusive, or suspicious accounts.

3. Credits, bookings, and cancellations

Users can earn PlayCred credits by completing rewarded ad experiences and may use those credits to book eligible sports venues and sessions through the platform.

Credits are issued only after PlayCred confirms successful ad completion. Credits have no cash value and cannot be refunded as money unless PlayCred expressly says otherwise.

Booking, cancellation, waiting-list, and refund rules may be shown in the app for each game. If you cancel a booking, any credit refund will depend on the rules shown for that booking.

PlayCred does not promise that watching ads will lead to a booking, reward outcome, or specific result beyond the rules shown in the app.
