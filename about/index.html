About PlayCred

Making sports more accessible through advertising-powered rewards.

PlayCred is a sports booking platform built to make playing easier, more affordable, and more accessible. We connect players who want to book games, brands that want meaningful attention, and venues that want more active bookings.

Instead of asking players to pay every time they want to play, PlayCred allows users to watch rewarded ads, earn PlayCred credits, and use those credits to book real sports sessions through the platform.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: turn advertising value into real playing opportunities.

We believe sports should not be limited only to people who can afford regular field bookings. Many players want to stay active, play with friends, and join games, but the cost of booking venues can become a barrier. PlayCred is designed to reduce that barrier.

Why PlayCred exists

In many cities, sports facilities are available, but they are not always affordable or easy to book regularly. At the same time, brands are spending money on digital advertising but often struggle to reach the right audience in a meaningful way.

PlayCred was created to solve both problems. Users give their attention to relevant advertising. Advertisers receive visibility from active users. That value is then converted into PlayCred credits that help users book sports sessions.

How the model works

  • Users watch rewarded ads and earn PlayCred credits.
  • Brands reach an audience that is already engaged and sports-focused.
  • Venues receive more visibility and more chances to fill available time slots.

FAQ

Questions from users and advertisers.

The answers below use the FAQ copy you provided and are grouped for easier scanning.

For Users

What is PlayCred?
PlayCred is a sports booking platform that allows users to earn PlayCred credits by watching rewarded ads and use those credits to book sports sessions.
How does PlayCred work?
The process is simple: watch ads, earn PlayCred credits, and use those credits to book available games or sports sessions through the PlayCred app.
Is PlayCred free to use?
Yes. Users can earn credits by watching rewarded ads and use those credits to book available sessions, subject to availability and platform rules.
What are PlayCred credits?
PlayCred credits are in-app credits used only within the PlayCred platform to book eligible sports sessions. They are not money, cash, cashback, or a financial wallet.
How do I earn PlayCred credits?
You can earn credits by watching completed rewarded video ads inside the PlayCred app. Once the ad is completed, credits are added to your account.
How many credits do I need to book a game?
The number of credits required may depend on the session type, location, and availability. The required credits will be clearly shown before you confirm your booking.
Can I buy PlayCred credits?
Currently, PlayCred is focused on allowing users to earn credits through ads. If credit purchase options become available in the future, they will be shown clearly in the app.
Do PlayCred credits expire?
Yes. PlayCred credits may have an expiry period. The expiry details will be shown in the app, and users are encouraged to use their credits before they expire.
Can I cancel my booking?
Cancellation rules depend on the platform policy shown at the time of booking. If your booking is eligible for a credit refund, the refundable amount will be displayed before you confirm cancellation.
What happens if a session is full?
If a session is full, you may not be able to book that slot. Where available, PlayCred may provide a waiting list or show alternative available sessions.
Can I transfer my credits to another user?
No. PlayCred credits are linked to your account and cannot be transferred, sold, exchanged, or redeemed for cash.
Are the ads skippable?
Rewarded ads may need to be watched fully in order to earn credits. If the ad is not completed, credits may not be issued.
What sports can I book on PlayCred?
PlayCred may offer access to football, basketball, and other sports sessions depending on availability, location, and partner venues.
Where is PlayCred available?
PlayCred is launching in selected locations first. Available sessions, venues, and cities will be shown inside the app.
Who can use PlayCred?
PlayCred is intended for users who meet the minimum age requirement and agree to the platform's Terms and Conditions.

For Advertisers

What is PlayCred for advertisers?
PlayCred gives brands the opportunity to reach an active sports-focused audience through rewarded video ads, banners, pop-ups, and other in-app advertising placements.
Why should advertisers use PlayCred?
PlayCred connects advertisers with users who are actively engaging with the platform to earn credits and book sports sessions. This creates a more focused and interactive advertising environment compared with standard passive ad placements.
What ad formats are available?
PlayCred may offer several advertising formats, including rewarded video ads, home screen banners, pop-up or interstitial ads, confirmation page banners, and other sponsored placements depending on the selected package.
What is a rewarded video ad?
A rewarded video ad is an ad that users watch in order to earn PlayCred credits. This format is designed to generate higher attention because users voluntarily watch the ad to receive credits.
Can advertisers choose where their ads appear?
Yes. Ad placement depends on the selected advertising package and available inventory. Placement options may include rewarded video, banner, pop-up, and confirmation screen positions.
How does campaign pricing work?
Campaign pricing may depend on the selected package, ad format, campaign duration, impressions, completed views, clicks, or other platform-based pricing shown at the time of purchase.
Are prices fixed?
Prices, packages, and campaign options may change from time to time. The latest available pricing will be shown on the PlayCred platform or in the advertiser dashboard.
Can I set a campaign budget?
Yes. Advertisers may be able to select or top up a campaign budget depending on the available campaign setup and package options.
What statistics can advertisers see?
Advertisers may be able to track key campaign metrics such as impressions, clicks, click-through rate, completed views, campaign status, and other performance data available in the advertiser dashboard.
Can I pause or edit my campaign?
Yes. Depending on the campaign settings, advertisers may be able to edit, pause, update dates, or adjust campaign details from the advertiser dashboard.
Does PlayCred approve ads before they go live?
Yes. Ads may be reviewed by PlayCred before going live to ensure they meet platform standards, technical requirements, and advertising guidelines.
What happens if my ad is rejected?
If an ad is rejected, the reason may be shown in the advertiser dashboard. Advertisers can then update the creative and resubmit it for review.
What type of businesses can advertise on PlayCred?
PlayCred is suitable for brands that want to reach active, young, social, and sports-interested users. This may include sports brands, fitness businesses, food and beverage companies, lifestyle brands, local services, events, and other relevant advertisers.
Can ads link to my website or landing page?
Yes. Clickable ads may direct users to the advertiser's website, landing page, app, or other approved destination.
Is PlayCred suitable for small businesses?
Yes. PlayCred can be suitable for both local businesses and larger brands that want to reach a targeted sports audience through digital advertising.
Can I run a short test campaign?
Yes. Subject to available packages, advertisers may be able to run a short campaign or pilot campaign before committing to a longer-term advertising plan.
Does PlayCred guarantee results?
PlayCred may provide campaign delivery estimates or performance reporting, but actual results may vary depending on the campaign, creative quality, audience engagement, targeting, and available inventory.
How can advertisers get started?
Advertisers can register on the PlayCred advertiser platform, select an available package, upload campaign creatives, and submit ads for review before launch.
What creative materials do I need?
Advertisers may need to provide videos, banners, images, campaign links, brand information, and any required text depending on the selected ad format.
Who can I contact for advertising support?
For advertising inquiries, campaign setup, or support, advertisers can contact PlayCred through the official contact form or support email shown on the website.
