About PlayCred

Making sports more accessible through advertising-powered rewards.

PlayCred is a sports booking platform built to make playing easier, more affordable, and more accessible. We connect players who want to book games, brands that want meaningful reach, and venues that want more action bookings.

Instead of raising players to pay more for the same seat or play, PlayCred allows access to world-class sports, at PlayCred venues, and earn rewards to book and play in the locations of your choice.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: turn advertising value into real playing opportunities.

We believe sports should not be limited by cost or location. By connecting players with advertisers and venues, we open the door to more access, more games, and more opportunities for everyone, everywhere.

Why PlayCred exists

In many places, sports facilities are unavailable, too far away, or cost more than they should. At the same time, brands are constantly looking for meaningful ways to reach real, active audiences.

PlayCred was created to solve both problems. We bridge that difference by aligning value with play.

How the model works

  • Users watch rewarded ads and earn PlayCred credits.
  • Brands reach real audiences that are actively engaged in sports.
  • Venues receive more visibility and more bookings through the platform.

FAQ

Questions from users and advertisers.

Here are some of the most common questions about PlayCred.

What is PlayCred?
PlayCred is a sports booking platform that allows users to earn PlayCred credits by watching rewarded ads and use those credits to book sports sessions.
How does PlayCred work?
Watch ads, earn PlayCred credits, and use those credits to book available games or sports sessions through the PlayCred app.
Is PlayCred free to use?
Yes. Users can earn credits by watching rewarded ads and use those credits to book available sessions, subject to availability and platform rules.
Can I buy PlayCred credits?
Currently, PlayCred is focused on allowing users to earn credits through ads. If credit purchase options become available, they will be shown clearly in the app.
